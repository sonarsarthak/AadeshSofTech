Privacy Policy

At AdeshSofTech Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Name and contact information

  • Website usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing our services and products

  • Processing payments and transactions

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

We may share your information with:

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data (where applicable)

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Provide personalized content
  • Remember user settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through our website or email.
